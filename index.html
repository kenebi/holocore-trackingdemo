Architecture

What's happening when you submit the form above:

User submits form
  ↓
HubSpot iframe → postMessage to parent window
  ↓
GTM Custom HTML listener catches message
  ↓
dataLayer.push({ event: 'form_submit', ... })
  ↓
GA4 Event tag fires (Custom Event trigger)
  ↓
form_submit recorded in GA4, marked as conversion
  ↓
(Parallel) HubSpot creates contact in Demo portal
